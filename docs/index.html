Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our free podcast creator

Is this service really completely free?

Yes! Our service is 100% free with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or API key requirements. We use browser-based text-to-speech technology and open-source solutions to provide unlimited podcast generation at no cost.

What file formats do you support?

We support PDF, DOCX (Microsoft Word), and TXT files. Our system automatically extracts text from these formats while preserving structure and formatting where possible.

How many voices are available?

We offer multiple voice options through browser-based synthesis and system voices. The exact number depends on your browser and operating system, but typically includes 6+ different voices with various accents and styles.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely! We prioritize your privacy. Document processing is done locally when possible, and we don't store your files or personal data. All processing is temporary and secure.

What's the maximum file size I can upload?

Currently, we support files up to 10MB in size. This accommodates most documents including lengthy research papers, books chapters, and detailed reports.

How long does it take to generate a podcast?

Generation time depends on document length and your device performance. Most documents are processed within 30 seconds to 2 minutes. Browser-based synthesis provides near-instant results.
