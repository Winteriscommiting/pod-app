Getting Started

How do I create my first podcast?

Simply visit our homepage, upload your document (PDF, DOCX, or TXT), choose a voice, and click generate. Your podcast will be ready to download in seconds!

Do I need to create an account?

No! You can use our basic features without any registration. Creating an account is optional and provides additional features like saving your podcasts and accessing your history.

Technical

What file formats are supported?

We support PDF files, Microsoft Word documents (.docx), and plain text files (.txt). Our system automatically extracts and processes the text content.

Why is the service completely free?

We believe in democratizing access to content creation tools. By using browser-based synthesis and open-source technologies, we can provide high-quality service without recurring costs.
