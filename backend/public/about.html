About PodcastAI

Making podcast creation accessible to everyone with completely free AI voice technology

Our Mission

We believe that everyone should have access to powerful content creation tools without financial barriers. PodcastAI was built to democratize podcast creation by providing completely free, high-quality text-to-speech conversion.

Our platform eliminates the need for expensive AI services, API subscriptions, or complex setup processes. We use browser-based synthesis and open-source technologies to ensure that podcast creation remains accessible to students, educators, content creators, and businesses of all sizes.

Technology Behind PodcastAI

We leverage cutting-edge web technologies and open-source solutions to provide enterprise-quality features at zero cost.

Browser Speech Synthesis

Utilizes the Web Speech API for instant, high-quality voice generation without server dependencies.

Smart Document Processing

Advanced text extraction from PDF, DOCX, and TXT files with structure preservation.
